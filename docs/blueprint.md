# **App Name**: Academia Horizon

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Homepage Content: Displays the main hero banner, quick links grid, and featured content blocks, with capabilities for content updates.
- Modular Information Pages: Serves dedicated pages for key institutional sections like Admissions, Academics, and Student Life, dynamically rendering rich content.
- News and Events Feed: Presents a scrollable list of the latest institutional news articles and upcoming events, each with a summary and a link to detailed content.
- Admissions & Inquiry Forms: Provides user-friendly forms for prospective students to request information or initiate their application process.
- Site-wide Search Engine: Implements a functional search bar that allows users to quickly find relevant information, courses, or personnel across the entire website.
- AI Campus Guide Tool: An interactive conversational tool that provides instant, personalized answers to frequently asked questions about programs, campus life, admissions, and faculty based on available site content.

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen visual scheme is light. The primary color is a deep, rich navy blue (#002046), selected for its association with authority, tradition, and academic depth. The background color is a soft, warm off-white (#FCF9F4), providing a classic and inviting canvas. The accent color is a light, serene greenish-blue (#C7EADE), subtly analogous to the primary and adding a touch of intellectual freshness and modern research alongside enduring tradition.
- Headline font: 'Newsreader' (serif), chosen for its elegant and authoritative character, embodying traditional academic values. Body and label font: 'Manrope' (sans-serif), providing a clean, modern, and highly readable foundation for extended text. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- All icons will be sourced from 'Material Symbols Outlined', ensuring a cohesive and contemporary visual language, adhering to the specified settings of 'FILL' 0, 'wght' 300, 'GRAD' 0, 'opsz' 24 for a lightweight, clear appearance.
- The layout features a prominent full-width hero section with a compelling text overlay, followed by a dynamic 'bento grid' for quick navigation. Content sections are presented with a clear hierarchy, including a three-column news and announcements feed, and visually distinct call-to-action blocks, all grounded by a multi-column, comprehensive footer, maintaining responsiveness across devices.
- Subtle and elegant hover effects will be implemented on interactive elements such as buttons, navigation links, quick links cards, and images. Image hovers will feature a gentle scaling ('group-hover:scale-105') with a smooth, duration-controlled transition to enhance engagement without distracting from the academic content.
